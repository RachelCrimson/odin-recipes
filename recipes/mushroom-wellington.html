Mushroom Wellington

Description

Potatoes, spinach and a saucy mushroom and onion mix wrapped in a crispy pastry. A hearty, vegetarian and vegan alternative to a beef wellington and a great meat-substitute for holiday dinners. Perfect for serving mutliple guests but also makes delicious leftovers. Best served with a vegetable gravy.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz cremini mushrooms
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 white onion
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 4 oz spinach
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • potato
  • 1 tbsp milk
