Onion Soup

Description

This hearty onion soup recipe is perfect for keeping you warm on cold days. It is an excuse to have your slow cooker running, filling your home with sweet, onion-smelling warmth. The cheesy bread balances the sweetness from the soup creating a rich and delicious combination. An added bonus is that the basic recipe is incredibly affordable! The cheese is the main price factor, meaning you can tailor this recipe to your budget by altering your choice of cheese. Minimal prep is required, the most time-consuming aspect being chopping onions. After that it's a simple case of letting the slow cooker do it's job. Then the most difficult part is waiting, especially with the delicious aromas to tempt you all day!
